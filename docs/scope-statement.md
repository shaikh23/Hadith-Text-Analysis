# Scope statement (v0.1)

## Intended scope

- Tradition focus: Sunni corpus, starting with **Sahih al-Bukhari**.
- Audience focus (initial): students and general readers who need traceable thematic lookup.
- Trust model: every record must show source reference, edition ID, and attributed grade claims when available.

## MVP boundaries

- English-only ingestion and retrieval for initial pilot.
- One edition (`eng-bukhari`) as source of truth for pilot records.
- Theme-first lookup with provenance, not legal adjudication.

## Out of scope (pilot)

- Independent authenticity re-grading.
- Arabic-first NLP, isnad graph analysis, or multilingual retrieval.
- Cross-collection deduplication and deep variant clustering.
